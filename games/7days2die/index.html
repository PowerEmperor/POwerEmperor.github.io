7 Days 2 Die

We just woke up in this terrible place! We have no clue how we got here and also why are only wearing underwear...

We got a strange note that told us that we didn't pay. As far as we know, we don't know any Duke. But this Duke stole our gear and left us for dead.

Our task now is to seek shelter and make some basic survival items! Or else we won't make it!

Lucky for us, we found a group of other strangers whom are helping us! This could be a good thing if we can trust each other...
